Privacy Policy

Tend · Last updated 30 June 2026

Tend (“we”, “us”) helps families coordinate care for a loved one. We take the privacy of that information seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We only collect what's needed to run the app:

  • Your account — your email address, used solely to sign you in with a one-time link. We do not store passwords.
  • Profile — the name and initials you choose.
  • Care information you add — the name of the person you're caring for, medications, tasks, appointments, reminder times, completion history, circle updates, and the “key info” you choose to save (such as doctor contacts, insurance references, and notes).

We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or device sensors.

How your information is used

Your information is used only to provide the service: to display your family's care space, send in-app reminders, and share entries with the members of your care circle. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Who can see your data

Care information is private to the members of your care circle — only people who have joined your space with its share code can see it. This access is enforced at the database level (row-level security).

Where it's stored

Data is stored securely with our infrastructure provider, Supabase, and transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account and care space are active, so your history (for example, how long a medication has been taken) remains available to you. You may request deletion of your account and its data at any time by contacting us.

Your choices

You can edit your entries in the app, sign out at any time, and request export or deletion of your data by email. Reminder notifications are optional and only appear if you grant permission.

Children

Tend is intended for adults coordinating care and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
